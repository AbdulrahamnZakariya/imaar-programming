SkillLens: AI-Powered Student Guidance and Hidden Talent Discovery

1. Introduction

SkillLens is an intelligent application that utilizes artificial intelligence to help students discover their hidden skills and receive personalized academic and career guidance based on data-driven insights.

2. The Problem We Address

  • Difficulty in Identifying Skills: Many students struggle to recognize their strengths and make informed career decisions.
  • Education-Workforce Gap: Traditional curricula do not always align with market needs, leaving graduates without the required skills.
  • Lack of Personalized Guidance: Students often rely on generic advice instead of tailored recommendations based on their potential.

3. Project Objectives

  • Analyze Student Performance: Using AI to uncover hidden talents.
  • Provide Personalized Career and Academic Pathways: Based on individual strengths and weaknesses.
  • Connect Students with Development Opportunities: Through specialized training programs.

6. Key Features

  • Comprehensive Analysis: Integrating academic and extracurricular data.
  • AI-Powered Skill Discovery: Uncovering hidden talents.
  • Personalized Learning and Career Recommendations.
  • Connection to Specialized Training Programs.
  • Smooth and Interactive User Experience.
